Why Self-Study Always Hits a Wall
◆
📚

Generic Courses That Ignore Your Weaknesses

N3以上のレベルになると、参考書やオンライン動画では対応しきれない文法や読解の壁にぶつかります。みんなと同じ教材では、自分の弱点が後回しになってしまうのです。汎用的なカリキュラムはあなたの状況に合っていません。だから伸び悩むのです。

Generic courses cannot address your specific gaps — progress stalls.
⏳

No Accountability, No Momentum

自己学習には締め切りも責任感もありません。忙しい日が続くと、ついサボってしまいます。一人で勉強すると、モチベーションを維持することが非常に難しくなります。毎週の目標を設定し、達成を確認してくれる存在がいれば、結果はまったく違います。

Without structure and accountability, motivation fades fast.
🗣️

Zero Real Speaking Practice

読んで書けるのに話せない — これはN3以上の学習者にとって最もよくある悩みです。テキストで文法を理解していても、実際の会話で使えなければ意味がありません。本物の日本語を話す機会がなければ、いつまでも「読めるだけ」のままです。

Reading and writing skills don't translate to real conversation without practice.
🎯

No Clear Path to Your JLPT Goal

試験まで何ヶ月あるのか、今どのレベルなのか、何を優先すべきなのか — 独学では戦略が見えにくくなります。N2やN1を目指すなら、逆算した学習計画が不可欠です。目標から逆算された、あなただけのロードマップが必要です。

Without a roadmap, exam preparation becomes guesswork and anxiety.

Questions Answered

よくある質問

Frequently Asked Questions
◆
Do I need to be at N3 level to join?
+

MTM Nihongo Coaching is optimised for N3 and above learners — those who have a solid foundation and want to push toward N2 or N1. If you're slightly below N3, contact us first. We'll do a free assessment and let you know if the programme is right for your current level or advise the best path forward.

How many sessions per week are included?
+

Session frequency is part of your personalised plan and depends on your timeline and availability. Typical learners have 2–3 instructor sessions per week, plus weekly coach check-ins. If you have a JLPT exam coming up within 3 months, we can increase intensity accordingly.

Are sessions online or in-person?
+

All sessions are conducted online via video call, which means you can learn from anywhere in Malaysia (or the world). Our fully online format was designed for working adults and students with busy schedules who still want a high-quality, structured learning experience.

What if I miss a session?
+

Life happens. We offer flexible rescheduling for sessions with 24-hour advance notice. Your personal coach will also help you adjust your plan to make up for any missed time without derailing your overall progress toward your exam date.

What exactly does the personal coach do?
+

Your personal coach is your accountability partner and learning strategist. They oversee your overall progress, adjust your study plan based on weekly performance, prepare your instructor for each lesson, and check in with you weekly to ensure you stay on track. They bridge the gap between knowing what to study and actually doing it consistently.

Can I see results before the JLPT exam?
+

Absolutely. Most students notice measurable improvements in reading speed, grammar accuracy, and listening comprehension within the first 4–6 weeks. Monthly progress reports document exactly how each skill area is developing, so you'll have clear evidence of your advancement throughout the programme.

How do I pay and are there instalment options?
+

Payment is made via bank transfer (Malaysia). We do offer instalment arrangements for the 6-month programme — contact us via WhatsApp to discuss your preferred payment structure. We want to make it as accessible as possible for committed learners.

How quickly can I start after signing up?
+

After your initial consultation and level assessment, we typically have your personalised plan ready within 5 business days and your first session booked within 7 days of enrolment. If you have an urgent JLPT deadline, let us know and we'll fast-track the onboarding process.
