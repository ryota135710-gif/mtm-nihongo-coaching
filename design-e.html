Breaking the Salary Ceiling: N2/N1 & Career Growth

In Malaysia, JLPT N2/N1 is the key to unlocking a higher Salary/Language Allowance and senior roles. However, the jump from N3 is where most self-study progress stalls. Textbooks alone can't bridge this gap. Without a strategic roadmap and expert coaching, you're not just struggling with grammar—you're leaving money on the table and delaying the career advancement you deserve.

Generic courses cannot address your specific gaps — progress stalls.

Keigo & Business Japanese

You can have casual conversations, but the moment you need to use keigo in a meeting or write a professional email, you freeze. Business Japanese requires a completely different register — and textbooks alone can't teach you the subtle hierarchy of politeness levels that Japanese professionals expect.

Without structure and accountability, motivation fades fast.

Can Read, Can't Speak

Your reading comprehension is decent. You can follow manga, articles, even some news. But when it's time to actually speak — your mind goes blank. You know the grammar, you know the vocabulary, yet the words won't come out naturally. The gap between your input skills and output skills keeps growing wider.

Reading and writing skills don't translate to real conversation without practice.

The Consistency Gap

You start with high motivation, but work gets busy and your textbooks gather dust. Without a strict environment or a coach to keep you accountable, long-term goals like N1/N2 remain "someday" dreams. You don't lack ability—you lack a system that ensures you show up every day.

Without a roadmap, exam preparation becomes guesswork and anxiety.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.
◆
What level of Japanese do I need to start?
+

Our program is designed for learners at JLPT N3 level and above. Our coaching system is most effective for intermediate and advanced learners who want to break through plateaus, master business Japanese, or achieve N2/N1 certification. If you're currently below N3, reach out and we'll discuss the best path forward for you.

How are lessons conducted?
+

All lessons and coaching sessions are conducted online via Zoom. You can join from anywhere with a stable internet connection. Sessions are scheduled at times convenient for you, including evenings and weekends.

What's the difference between a "lesson" and a "coaching session"?
+

Lessons focus on teaching new content — grammar, vocabulary, reading, and speaking practice. Coaching sessions focus on reviewing your progress, adjusting your study plan, addressing challenges, and keeping you accountable. Together, they form a complete system.

Can I choose my lesson schedule?
+

Yes. We offer flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends. Your schedule is arranged directly with your instructor to fit your lifestyle.

Is the daily chat support really available every day?
+

Yes. Your coach is available via chat 7 days a week. Response times are typically within a few hours during business hours. You can ask questions, submit assignments, or simply check in with your progress.

Do you offer a trial lesson?
+

We offer a free 30-minute consultation session where we assess your level and discuss your goals. This is not a sales pitch — it's a genuine assessment to see if we're the right fit for your learning journey.

What payment methods do you accept?
+

We accept bank transfer and major payment methods available in Malaysia. Payment plans can be discussed during your consultation.
